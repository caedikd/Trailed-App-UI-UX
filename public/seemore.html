River-Creek Trail

Distance: 10km

Elevation: 10km

Rating: 3/5

Features:

"long windy trails"

"beautiful scenic river but it's hard to get to"

"steep inclines"

Reviews:

Laura Ingalls

Possibly my favorite Yosemite trail, this one is not for the faint of heart! Don't let the distance fool you into thinking this a beginner friendly trail there are many sharp rocks and steep inclines but at the end there is a beautiful creek that makes it all worth it!

Mary Ingalls

By far the most gorgeous Yosemite trail! Best for intermediate backpackers and above but don't shy away from going with a group!
